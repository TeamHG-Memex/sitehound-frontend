Search URLs

expand_more expand_less

You can search with Google and Bing for more URLs relevant to the domain based on the keywords previously defined

  • Each time you Fetch URLs, you will get a batch of 150-200 results from several Search Engines
  • Results are initially labeled as neutral, it's up to you to label them as relevant or irrelevant.
  • You don't have to label all the results, just keep going until you think you teach the system enough of what you are looking for, though we'd recommend at least 100, but could be more depending on the nuance of the domains.
  • In the results view, there is an infinite scroll, so take it easy :)
